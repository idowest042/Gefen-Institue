About Gefen Institute

Empowering Global Education

The Gefen Institute is dedicated to bridging educational gaps by connecting talented students with fully funded scholarship opportunities in Brazil's prestigious universities.

Founded with a vision to create global citizens, we provide comprehensive support services that include intensive Portuguese language training, cultural immersion programs, and personalized guidance throughout the scholarship application process.

Our team of experienced educators and advisors understands the challenges international students face and works tirelessly to ensure a smooth transition to academic life in Brazil.

Our Mission

To democratize access to quality higher education by connecting talented students from around the world with fully funded scholarship opportunities in Brazil, while fostering cross-cultural understanding and global citizenship.

Our Story

From Vision to Reality

Gefen Institute was founded by Professor Godson Igwe, whose vision was to open doors for Nigerian students to study in Brazil. With a strong background in Portuguese language instruction and international collaboration, he has guided countless students through the PEC-G and PEC-PG scholarship programs. Over the years, we’ve grown into a trusted center where students not only prepare for scholarships but also gain the cultural and linguistic skills needed to succeed in Brazil. Professor Igwe’s leadership continues to shape Gefen Institute’s mission, helping students achieve their academic and professional goals abroad.
